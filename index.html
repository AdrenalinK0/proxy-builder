‚ÑπÔ∏è How Chain Proxy Works

A chain proxy routes your traffic through two proxies in sequence:

You ‚Üí Config 1 (Worker/CDN) ‚Üí Config 2 (Chain) ‚Üí Internet

This fixes your outgoing IP address to the chain proxy's IP, ensuring a consistent IP for all destinations.

Supported Protocols

  • VLESS ‚Äî vless://uuid@server:port?params
  • VMess ‚Äî vmess://base64-json
  • Trojan ‚Äî trojan://password@server:port?params
  • Shadowsocks ‚Äî ss://base64(method:pass)@server:port
